Introduction

Digi Gal is a play on the word digital, which involves or relates to the use of computer technology. I am adaptable. I value helping others and designing creative solutions for everyday obstacles. I enjoy homeschooling my children, volunteering and learning about tech. My curiousity has propelled my desire to learn about new web design concepts. I completed a Fullstack Web Development bootcamp providing over 400 hours of hands on training. I am currently enrolled in a nanodegree program studying Front End.
